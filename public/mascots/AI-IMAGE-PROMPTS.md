# Shark Human Alliance — AI Image Generation Prompts

## Style Reference (Base Prompt)

Every prompt below should be **prepended** with this style block to keep all images consistent:

> **STYLE:** Cute kawaii chibi-style digital illustration with soft watercolor texture. Two anthropomorphic cartoon sharks standing upright on short stubby legs. Rounded soft bodies, large heads with big expressive anime-style blue eyes with white sparkle highlights. White belly area, smooth skin. Pastel color palette. Soft underwater background with light rays from above, translucent bubbles, faint fish silhouettes, subtle seaweed at bottom edges. Clean lines, gentle shading, no hard shadows. Warm, friendly, slightly humorous tone. High quality, 16:9 aspect ratio unless noted.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Consistency:** Always include the STYLE BLOCK at the beginning of each prompt
2. **Generator:** These prompts are optimized for **DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT) and **Midjourney v6+**
3. **For Midjourney:** Add `--style raw --s 200 --q 2` at the end for best results
4. **For DALL-E 3:** Use "vivid" style setting, not "natural"
5. **Negative prompt** (if supported): `realistic, photographic, 3D render, dark, scary, teeth showing aggressively, horror, blood, violent`
6. **Color calibration:** After generating, check that Finnley is **bright blue** and Luna is **teal/cyan** — regenerate if colors drift
7. **Background removal:** For web use, generate on simple backgrounds and use remove.bg or similar tool to get transparent PNGs
8. **Batch generation:** Generate 4 variants of each prompt and pick the best one for consistency
